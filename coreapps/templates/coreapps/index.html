The heavens themselves, the planets and this centre earth, Observe degree, priority and place, Insisture, course, proportion, season, form, Office and custom, in all line of order;


But when the planets in evil mixture to disorder wander,


What plagues and what portents! what mutiny! What raging of the sea! shaking of earth! Commotion in the winds! frights, changes, horrors, Divert and crack, rend and deracinate The unity and married calm of states Quite from their fixure!


O, when degree is shaked, Which is the ladder to all high designs, Then enterprise is sick!


How could communities, Degrees in schools and brotherhoods in cities, Peaceful commerce from dividable shores, The primogenitive and due of birth, Prerogative of age, crowns, sceptres, laurels, But by degree, stand in authentic place? Take but degree away, untune that string, And, hark, what discord follows!
